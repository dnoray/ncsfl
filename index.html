About

The Northeast Christian School Football League (NCSFL) Tournament is open to Christian Schools. This tournament is free and will be a great opportunity for teams to compete and fellowship together during the two days of competition.

Tournament Format

You will play a full schedule at the tournament. Last year, we hosted a total of 6 teams. The tournament consists of a Round Robin seeding with an Elimination Bracket after seeding has been completed.

There will be two fields with consecutive games being played simultaneously. Some teams will have the opportunity to play under the lights.

Seeding & Play In Games

 

The Round Robin games are designed to appropriately seed teams in the elimination bracket. All teams will play a shortened game and will then compete in a play in game if necessary. The Round Robin and play in games will be 20 minute halves with 3 timeouts for the entire game.

Elimination Bracket

15 Minute Quarters
3 Timeouts per Half

After being seeded, teams will compete and move forward in the bracket toward the championship game under the lights. If a team loses in the elimination bracket, they will no longer have opportunity to compete for first place.

You won't want to miss this year's tournament. Contact us today if you are interested in bringing your team to compete this year.

Tournament Rules

The NCSFL Tournament is played as an 8-man, controlled contact style with 3 linemen, 2 receivers, 2 backs, and a QB. Blocking is allowed with arms in or out, but rough blocking is not allowed. Uniforms will vary for each opponent, but each player's uniform should match the entire team. A home and away jersey is recommended, but not required. Flags should be a distinctly different color than the bottoms of your uniform. The suggested flags are from Flag-a-tag because they "pop" when flags are pulled which help in identifying the timing of a flag pull. Tournament rules can be read at length in the Official NCSFL Tournament Rules.
